About FLP

🏢 Company Overview

Forever Living Products International (FLP) was founded in 1978 by Rex Maughan in Scottsdale, Arizona, USA. It is one of the world's largest growers, manufacturers, and distributors of aloe vera-based health, beauty, and wellness products.

🌿 Our Mission

To improve the quality of life for people around the world through pure, natural aloe vera products and a proven business opportunity. FLP is committed to sustainability, quality, and the wellbeing of every distributor and customer.

📍 Headquarters

Scottsdale, Arizona, USA
Regional offices across all major continents. Operations in 160+ countries with a growing presence in India, Asia, Africa, and the Middle East.

Marketing Plan & Earnings

📈 Business Model

FLP operates on a multi-level marketing (MLM) / direct selling model. Distributors earn through personal retail sales and by building a team of active distributors beneath them (downline).

💵 Income Streams

  • Retail profit on product sales
  • Personal bonus (up to 18%)
  • Group volume bonus (up to 13%)
  • Leadership bonus & Profit sharing
  • Car plan & Travel awards

🎖️ Ranks / Levels

  • Distributor (New Joiner)
  • Assistant Supervisor
  • Supervisor
  • Assistant Manager
  • Manager → Senior Manager
  • Soaring Manager & Above
